Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia apetizzer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your favourite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarms water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
      • For the topping:
    • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
    • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
    • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
    • 10 ounves brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
    • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

    Method

    1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need actually knead the dough, though I spmetimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
